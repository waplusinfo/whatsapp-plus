What is WhatsApp Plus?

WhatsApp Plus is a modified version of WhatsApp Messenger designed for users who want deeper customization, enhanced privacy controls, and advanced messaging features. It offers an expanded set of tools that go beyond the limitations of the official WhatsApp app.

Users can personalize the interface with themes, change colors and fonts, hide online status, disable read receipts selectively, increase media upload limits, send images without compression, and much more.

Note: WhatsApp Plus is not an official app and is not affiliated with WhatsApp Inc.

Key Features

Advanced Privacy

Hide online status, freeze last seen, disable read receipts, and control your visibility.

Customization

Choose from thousands of themes, change UI colors, fonts, icons, and chat bubble styles.

Improved Media Sharing

Send high-resolution photos, videos up to 2GB, and share files without compression.

Productivity Tools

Auto-reply, message scheduling, broadcast enhancements, and advanced group settings.
